Escape HackerYou!

Uh oh! After a long day of dev stuff, HackerMe must have fallen asleep in the building. Upon waking up, it was discovered that everyone else has left, locking you in!

  • Use the arrow keys on your computer's keyboard to guide HackerMe through the map
  • Collect the coloured keys to use with the corresponding coloured locks and access restricted areas
  • Gather up all the HackerChips to unlock the elevator
  • Once finished, get to the elevator and escape!

You Did It!

Congratulations, you completed the level! Luckily for you, though perhaps not for HackerMe, more levels will be created in the future.

** I cannot take credit for the game mechanics of "Escape HackerYou". I was influenced heavily by a game called "Chip's Challenge" that I played often as a kid on my family's Windows '98 computer. **
